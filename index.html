who am i?

Helloooo! I'm Kirsten Hattori, a front end web developer driven by learning and skill development in all facets of life. A creative problem solver with a keen eye for detail who enjoys writing clean, responsive, and accessible code while drawing on knowledge of UX design principles and practices.

With a professional background in retail and e-commerce on teams from customer service, merchandizing, order fulfillment, and business operations combined with a passion for shopping small, independent, and local I would like to work with independent, small, and local businesses and creators to enhance their online presence.

My education before bootcamp focused on interdisciplinary studies with a focus on psychology and sociology studying towards a profession in special education. My own experience with special education inspired this desire in me to help learners of all abilities make their strengths and weaknesses work for them.

Outside of learning, my interests include knitting, skiing, gardening, jigsaw and sudoku puzzles, and hiking with my doggo, Murphy.
